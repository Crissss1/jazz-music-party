The Band.

§ 02 / Artists

A Leeds-born jazz collective formed in 2025. Traditional jazz, fusion, soul, Latin and pop re-imagined — six players who take incredible care over every note. Expect drum solos, sax flights, and a vocalist who lands somewhere between Nina Simone and Amy Winehouse.
